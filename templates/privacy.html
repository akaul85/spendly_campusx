Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you register, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access. Your account is protected by the password you set — please use a strong, unique password. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app; these providers are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond within a reasonable time.
